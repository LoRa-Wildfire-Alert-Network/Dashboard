## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a vulnerability, please email [admin@lorawildfirealertnet.work](mailto:admin@lorawildfirealertnet.work) directly rather than opening a public issue.
You can expect an initial response within 48 hours. If the vulnerability is confirmed, we will work on a fix and credit you in the release notes. 
If declined, we will explain our reasoning.
