# Alerts

The dashboard can notify you when sensor conditions are met on nodes you subscribe to. Alerts are created automatically when you subscribe to a node.

## How Alerts Work

When you **subscribe to a node**, the dashboard creates default alert preferences for that node:

- **Smoke detected** — Alert when smoke is detected
- **Temperature above 70°C** — Alert when temperature exceeds 70°C
- **Battery below 20%** — Alert when battery drops below 20%

If these conditions are met, an alert is recorded and you will receive an email notification to the email used during account sign-up.

## Filter vs. Alerts

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Alerts** (notifications) | Notify you when conditions are met on subscribed nodes. Stored in the dashboard will trigger email |
| **Filter panel "Alerts"** | Filter the Node List to *show* nodes that match conditions (smoke, temp, humidity, battery). Does not send notifications |

Use the [filter panel](filter-nodes.md) to focus on nodes with smoke, high temp, low humidity, or low battery. Use subscriptions (and their default alert preferences) to receive notifications for nodes you care about.

## Email Notifications

You will receive messages when alert conditions are met. Contact your administrator if you expect email alerts but are not receiving them.
