# How to Subscribe to Nodes

Subscribing to a node adds it to your monitored list. Subscribed nodes appear on the map and trigger alert notifications for you.

> **Permission required**: Your organization role must have the **Subscribe to nodes** permission. If the subscribe button is not visible, contact your org admin.

## Subscribe from a Node Card

1. In the **Node List** panel on the right, click a node card to expand it
2. Click the **Subscribe** button that appears in the expanded card
3. The button updates to show you are now subscribed

## Unsubscribe from a Node

1. Click the node card to expand it
2. Click the **Unsubscribe** button
3. The node is removed from your subscriptions

## What Subscriptions Do

- **Map**: Only subscribed nodes appear on the map
- **Filtering**: Use the "Only show subscribed nodes" filter to limit the node list to your subscriptions
- **Alerts**: Subscribing enables alert notifications for that node (smoke, high temp, battery < 20%). See [Alerts](alerts.md) for details
- **Acknowledge**: Only subscribers can acknowledge alerts on a node
- **Persistence**: Subscriptions are saved to your account and persist across sessions
