# Complete Your Account Setup

If you signed up with a username only, you need to add authentication to your account. This secures your account and is required to enter back into the Dashboard.

## Why Add Authentication?

- **Access** — Without an authentication method, you will be unable to log back into the Dashboard

## How to Add Your Email

1. Sign in to the LoRa Wildfire Dashboard
2. Click your **profile icon** (avatar) in the top-right corner
3. Select **Manage account**
4. In the **Profile** section, click **Add email address**
5. Enter your email address
6. Check your inbox for a **verification code** or **verification link**
7. Enter the code or click the link to verify your email

Your account is now fully set up.

## Add Two-Factor Authentication (Optional)

For extra security, you can enable two-factor authentication (2FA). See [Manage your account — Add Two-Factor Authentication](../how-to/manage-account.md#add-two-factor-authentication-2fa) for step-by-step instructions.

## Troubleshooting

- **Didn't receive the verification email?** Check your spam folder. You can request a new code from the account page
- **Verification link expired?** Links expire after about 24 hours. Request a new one
