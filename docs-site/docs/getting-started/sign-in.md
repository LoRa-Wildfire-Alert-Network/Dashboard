# Sign In

You need to sign in to use the LoRa Wildfire Dashboard.

## How to Sign In

1. Open the dashboard in your browser.
2. Click **Sign In** in the top-right corner.
3. Enter your username and proceed with the authentication method you enabled upon sign-up.
4. After signing in, you'll see the dashboard with the map and node list.

## Create an Account

If you don't have an account:

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.
2. Select a Provider to log in with (e.g. Google, Microsoft, etc) or enter your first name, last name, and username.
4. Complete the sign-up flow.
5. You'll be signed in and taken to the dashboard.

### If You Signed Up with Username Only

If you created your account with just a username, you need to **add authentication** to complete your account setup. This helps secure your account and is required to enter back into the Dashboard.

1. Click your **profile icon** (avatar) in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Manage account**
3. In the **Profile** section, click **Add email address**.
4. **Add your email address** and verify it (you'll receive a verification code or link).
5. Optionally enable two-factor authentication for extra security.

Once your email is verified, your account is fully set up. See [Complete Your Account Setup](complete-account-setup.md) for more details.

## Organizations

If your account is part of an organization, use the **Organization Switcher** in the navbar to switch between your personal account and any organizations you belong to. See [Organizations](../how-to/organizations.md) for how to create organizations, add members, and manage them.

## Sign Out

Click your profile icon (avatar) in the top-right corner, then choose **Sign out**.

## Manage Your Account

To update your profile picture, username, add two-factor authentication, or create a passkey, see [Manage your account](../how-to/manage-account.md).
